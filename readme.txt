Ethio Photo Editor is a Full featured picture editor, that lets you to Crop, Edit, Filter, Draw and Write text in Amharic and English on your photos. Add a Sticker, Emoji and Image to your picture and share it via on your favorite social media.

KEY FEATURES
• Full featured picture editor.
• Apply Photo Effects.
• Apply Image Cropping: Crop, Rotate, Scale.
• Tune image: Brightness, Contrast, Saturation.
• Write or Draw cool text over photos.
• Add Sticker, Emoji and Image to your picture.
• Upload and Edit images from gallery or camera.
• Save pictures and download to gallery.
• Share on your favorite social media.
